Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Easy Table: Smart Web Data Extractor. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how our browser extension operates and why you can trust us.

2. Zero Data Collection Policy

Easy Table is a "Local-First" application.

  • No Data Uploads: All table extraction and conversion happen entirely within your browser. We never transmit your table content to our servers.
  • No Personal Identification: We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or browsing history.
  • No Account Required: No login or registration is needed to use all features.

3. Use of Permissions

  • activeTab Used to detect and highlight tables on the page only when the user interacts with the extension.
  • storage Used to securely save your extraction history and preference settings locally in your browser.
  • scripting Used to inject the extraction logic and the "Smart Hover" effect into the current webpage.

4. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser cache will permanently delete all saved history.
