Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Easy Table: Smart Web Data Extractor. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how our browser extension operates and why you can trust us with your data.

2. Zero Data Collection Policy

Easy Table is a "Local-First" application.

  • No Table Data Uploads: All table extraction, editing, and conversion happen entirely within your browser. We never transmit your table content to our servers.
  • No Personal Identification: We do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or browsing history.
  • No Account Required: You can use all features of the extension without creating an account.

3. Use of Permissions

To provide its core functionality, Easy Table requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab Used to detect and highlight tables on the page you are currently viewing.
  • storage Used to save your last 10 extractions and settings locally on your device.
  • scripting Used to inject the extraction logic and the "Smart Hover" highlight effect into the webpage.

4. Third-Party Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (such as Google Analytics 4) to track aggregate usage patterns (e.g., how many users clicked the "Export to XLSX" button). This data is completely de-identified and is used solely to improve the user experience.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage, you have absolute control. Uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser cache will instantly and permanently delete all saved table history.
